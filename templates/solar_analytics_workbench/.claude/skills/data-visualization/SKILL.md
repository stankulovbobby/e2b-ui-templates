# Data Visualization Skill

Every chart should have:
- a clear business question
- readable labels
- units where relevant
- realistic mock values
- a nearby explanation or insight

Prefer simple native SVG/CSS charts for this MVP.
Use color intentionally:
- blue for core volume or revenue
- green for healthy performance
- amber for watch items
- red for risk or escalation

Do not create decorative charts that lack business meaning.
