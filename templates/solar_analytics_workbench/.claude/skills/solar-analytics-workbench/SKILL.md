# Solar Analytics Workbench Skill

The page should help an operations leader answer:
- Where are projects slowing down?
- Which AHJs or utilities are causing delay?
- Which stages have the longest tail, not just the highest average?
- Which projects need coordinator or executive escalation?
- What should the team do next?

Recommended sections:
1. Metric summary for median cycle time, permit aging, PTO lag, and sample size.
2. Distribution or histogram views for cycle time and permit aging.
3. Trend chart for monthly movement.
4. Stage aging benchmark with median and P90.
5. AHJ or utility benchmark table.
6. Insight cards with plain-English recommendations.
7. Drill-down project table.

Do not show charts without explaining what the reader should conclude.
