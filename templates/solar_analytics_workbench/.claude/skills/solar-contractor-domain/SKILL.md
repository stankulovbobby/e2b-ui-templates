# Solar Contractor Domain Skill

Use domain-native language for solar and home-services contractors.

Prefer entities and terms such as:
- homeowner
- lead source
- signed contract
- site survey
- design
- AHJ
- permit submitted
- permit approved
- scheduled install
- crew capacity
- inspection
- PTO
- utility interconnection
- battery attachment
- adders
- finance approval
- change order
- cycle time
- at-risk project

Avoid generic placeholder language such as `Customer A`, `Metric 1`, or `Project 123` when realistic solar examples would be better.
