# Data Visualization Skill

Every chart should answer a business question.

Good chart usage:

- Line chart: trend over time, such as signed revenue or installed revenue.
- Bar chart: stage breakdown, regional comparison, team leaderboard.
- Funnel/pipeline: movement from lead to signed contract.
- KPI card: a value, delta, and short explanation.
- Table: concrete records that explain the aggregate metric.
- Insight card: interpretation and recommended action.

Rules:

- Include labels, units, and plain-English subtitles.
- Pair visuals with short explanations.
- Avoid decorative charts that do not clarify a decision.
- Use mock numbers that are internally plausible.
- Prefer native SVG/CSS for the MVP unless a dependency is already included.
