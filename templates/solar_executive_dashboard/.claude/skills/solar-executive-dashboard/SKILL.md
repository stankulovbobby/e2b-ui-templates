# Solar Executive Dashboard Skill

The page should help an executive understand the business in under 10 seconds.

Prioritize:

- signed revenue
- pipeline value
- close rate
- proposal-to-signed conversion
- install capacity
- cycle time
- permit backlog
- inspection pass rate
- PTO aging
- at-risk projects
- regional performance
- rep or crew leaderboard
- battery attachment rate

Recommended layout:

1. Hero summary with an opinionated headline.
2. Four to six KPI cards above the fold.
3. Revenue or pipeline trend chart.
4. Operations health section.
5. Bottleneck/insight cards with plain-English recommendations.
6. At-risk jobs table.
7. Sales or crew performance leaderboard.

Write for business decision-makers, not developers. Prefer direct phrases like "Permitting is now the constraint" over vague labels like "Operational analytics".
