# Solar Contractor Domain Skill

Use domain-native concepts that are credible for solar/home-services contractors.

Good entities:

- homeowner
- lead
- proposal
- financing application
- signed contract
- site survey
- system design
- AHJ
- permit packet
- crew
- inspection
- PTO
- utility interconnection
- battery attachment
- adders
- MPU
- subcontractor
- change order

Common stage language:

1. New Lead
2. Qualified
3. Proposal Sent
4. Financing
5. Signed
6. Site Survey
7. Design
8. Permit Submitted
9. Permit Approved
10. Scheduled
11. Installed
12. Inspection
13. PTO
14. Complete

Avoid generic placeholder language such as `Customer A`, `Metric 1`, `Task status`, or `Project #001` unless it is surrounded by realistic domain context.

The output should make sense to a solar owner, VP of sales, operations manager, permitting lead, or installation director.
